PGH / DIGS

Illustrating One Pittsburgh House At A Time

  • ABOUT

PGH / Digs is an art and architectural documentary project started in 2013 by Pittsburgh graphic designer and illustrator Mundania Horvath, founder of design blog Steeltown Anthem and owner of design studio Reconstructing Ideas. PGH / Digs documents residential homes in the city of Pittsburgh through original graphic illustrations created by Mundania and featured on PGHdigs.com.


The concept for the project was born during Mundania’s time at the local architectural firm moss architects. While observing hands-on drafting from her co-workers, she was inspired to connect her passion for various architectural styles, structural history and facades with her own illustrative artwork. “I find beauty in the historic and the modern,” said Mundania, whose creative inspirations come from old textbooks, ghost signs, vintage cameras and geometry. From ranch to row house, Mundania brings her design aesthetic to the homes and communities of Pittsburgh, one house at a time.
